FAQ

What’s included in the animation package?
Each project includes 3D modeling (if needed), texturing, lighting, animation, rendering, and final delivery in high quality. We handle the full visual process to ensure your product or concept looks cinematic and realistic.
How long does a project usually take?
Most product or CGI animations take 6–8 days, depending on the complexity, duration, and level of detail. Architectural and AI-based projects may take slightly longer
Do you offer revisions?
Yes. Every project includes a set number of revisions to make sure the final result matches your vision and brand style.
Can I hire you for a single short animation?
Absolutely. You can order short-form animations, single product shots, or custom-length projects based on your needs.
What types of animations do you create?
Product animations, CGI ads (3D + real-world integration), Architectural visualization, AI + 3D animations, Social media ad Posters
What do you need from the client to start?
Usually we need: Product images or dimensions, Brand guidelines (if available), Reference style or idea, Script or concept (optional), If you don’t have these, we can help develop the concept.
How do payments work?
Projects typically start with an 50% advance payment, and the remaining amount is paid before final delivery.
Do you work with international clients?
Yes. dotfps works with clients worldwide and delivers projects fully online.
